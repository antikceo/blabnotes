BlabNotes Transcription with Google Web Speech API

  

Custom Commands

You can say:

"end paragraph" for new line

"end sentence" for '.'

"question mark" for '?'

"comma" for ','

"insert exclamation" for '!'

"begin quote" for '"'

"end quote" for '"'

"insert question mark" for a period

Custom Commands

"delete last" to delete a word

"delete all" to clear the canvas

"send me away" to open 'Export' dialog

"stop listening" to turn off microphone
